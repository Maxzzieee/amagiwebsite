02 / The Problem

The Mandate Is Set.
The Infrastructure Isn't.

Singapore has legislated a full EV transition. The commercial fleet operators tasked with executing it have no integrated partner to make it happen.

Upfront Cost

EV buses cost S$250K–S$400K each. Fleet operators lack capital access and structured financing pathways.

Supply Chain Fragmentation

Vehicles, chargers, and parts come from separate vendors with no single point of accountability.

Charging Infrastructure

Depot-scale fast charging requires solar integration and grid upgrades that operators can't self-fund or manage.

Operator Complexity

Operators must manage LTA compliance, OEM relationships, and software platforms simultaneously — without dedicated EV expertise.

Singapore needs to electrify 97% of its commercial bus fleet in under 6 years. The infrastructure gap is structural, not cyclical.
